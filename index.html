Frequently Asked Questions

How does Vestora Finance ensure my stocks are real?

All stocks on our platform are purchased 1:1 through licensed custodians and tokenized on the blockchain. Each token represents actual ownership of the underlying asset, registered in your name, with full voting rights and dividend entitlements.

Can I trade stocks from any global market?

Yes! Vestora Finance provides access to stocks from major global markets including NYSE, NASDAQ, LSE, TSE, and many more. Our platform removes geographical restrictions, allowing you to diversify your portfolio internationally.

What are the fees compared to traditional brokers?

Our fees are typically 50-80% lower than traditional brokerages. We charge a small transaction fee for trades and no hidden fees. The exact fee structure will be published before our public launch.

Is Vestora Finance regulated?

Yes, we work with regulated custodians and comply with all relevant financial regulations. We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of security, compliance, and transparency.

What is real-world asset tokenization and how does it work?

Real-world asset tokenization is the process of converting ownership rights of a physical or traditional financial asset (like stocks) into digital tokens on a blockchain. At Vestora Finance, we work with licensed custodians who purchase and hold the actual stocks in your name. We then issue digital tokens that represent your ownership of these assets on a 1:1 basis. This allows for fractional ownership, instant transfers, and 24/7 trading while maintaining all the rights and benefits of traditional stock ownership.
